About The Project

“Library System” is designed to act as a centralized platform for libraries to manage their whole operations. These will serve as a smart management tool especially in dealing with records in libraries.

Can't do enough online shopping! No pesky sales people and it's so much fun to get presents delivered to my door.

A library is a collection of books, and possibly other materials and media, that is accessible for use by its members and members of allied institutions.

A library database system is an infrastructure that allows users to search books and book content, add/remove, and download selected books.
